Alasdair Koplick has spent the last 8 years working in live sound, first as a hobby through school and university societies, and then later in a professional capacity, running sound for Liar, The Bitch and The Wardobe at the Turbine Theatre in Battersea. He has experience mixing live sound for bands, plays, pantomines, small-scale drama productions, and large scale musicals with live bands. He also has extensive sound design and mixing experience through his music career under the alias Aeix , some examples of which can be found below from the last musical he worked on; Alice by Heart at the University of Manchester. He can be contacted via email or phone for live sound or mixing related inquires.

Alasdair has experience with the X32 & M32, Yamaha MG16, Yamaha TF5, Yamaha LS9 mixing consoles, as well as the ETC Ion, ETC ColorSource and QLab (from most to least experience). He is always looking to expand his experience and is familiar with rigging and foccusing lights, as well as programming for the ETC Ion, and creating scenes to handle DCAs for complex shows using TheatreMix.
