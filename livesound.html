Alasdair Koplick has spent the last 8 years working in live sound, first as a hobby through school and university societies, and then later in a professional capacity, running sound for Liar, The Bitch and The Wardobe at the Turbine Theatre in Battersea. He has experience mixing live sound for bands, plays, pantomines, small-scale drama productions, and large scale musicals with live bands. He also does sound design and mix engineering, some examples of which can be found below from the last musical he worked on; Alice by Heart at the University of Manchester. He can be contacted via email or phone for live sound or mixing related inquires.
